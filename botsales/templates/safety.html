Trust & Safety

Your safety is our priority. Learn how to buy and sell safely on BotSales.

Watch Out for Scams

Never send payment before seeing the robot in person. If a deal seems too good to be true, it probably is. Report suspicious listings immediately.

Tips for Buyers

  • Always meet in a public place to inspect the robot
  • Test the robot before paying
  • Never wire money or use gift cards as payment
  • Use our in-app messaging to keep records
  • Check seller reviews and verification status
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels off, walk away

Tips for Sellers

  • Meet buyers in safe, public locations
  • Accept only cash or secure payment methods
  • Never ship before receiving payment
  • Keep copies of all communications
  • Be wary of buyers offering more than asking price
  • Report suspicious inquiries

How We Protect You

  • Verified seller program for trusted dealers
  • Secure in-platform messaging
  • Fraud detection systems
  • 24/7 report monitoring
  • Masked phone numbers for privacy
  • Dedicated trust & safety team

Report a Problem

  • Click the "Report" button on any listing
  • Email us at safety@botsales.com
  • Call our hotline: 1800 BOT SAFE
  • Reports are reviewed within 24 hours
  • We take action against fraudulent accounts
