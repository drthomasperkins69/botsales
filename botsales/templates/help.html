Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a listing?

Click the "Sell Robot" button in the header, fill in your robot's details, upload photos, and publish. Your listing will be live immediately.

Is it free to list a robot?

Yes! Basic listings are completely free. We also offer featured listings with premium placement for a small fee.

How do I contact a seller?

Click the "Contact Seller" button on any listing page. You'll need to be logged in to send messages.

How do I report a suspicious listing?

Click the "Report" button on the listing page and select a reason. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours.
